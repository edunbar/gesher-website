Target Case Study Competition

Registration due by Wednesday February 1st

This quarter we have the privilege of working with Target, the large scale retailer of quality goods for lower prices. Their mission statement: "Our mission is to make Target your preferred shopping destination in all channels by delivering outstanding value, continuous innovation and exceptional guest experiences by consistently fulfilling our Expect More. Pay Less. Brand promise." Visit corporate.target.com/about for more information on the company!

Target is sponsoring us to host our very first case study competition in which teams of 2-5 students will partner up to solve a case study scenario given by Target. Each team will choose one of two case study problems and present their solutions to a panel of Target representatives near the end of the quarter where the panel will choose the winning group that solved their case study with the greatest evidence, relevance, and execution.

This event gives you as students an opportunity to apply their professional and educational skills, while expanding their networks by meeting Target representatives, working in collaboration with each other, and meeting other student teams. The first place team will receive $1,000, second place will get $750, and the team in third place will be rewarded $200. We will be hosting the kickoff event on Thursday, February 2nd, at Engineering 2 Room 499 from 5:30pm-6:30pm where teams will receive more information on the competition, rules, and expectations. There will also be Target representatives at the meeting answering any questions.

The DEADLINE to register is Wednesday February 1st, which is one day before the kickoff meeting. If some team members cannot make the first meeting we understand, so teams are encouraged to contact us at www.geshergroup.org/contact to let us know which members will be absent.

See you there!
